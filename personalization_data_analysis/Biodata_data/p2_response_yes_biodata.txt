The hash join is a method of joining two relations, where one relation is partitioned into smaller pieces and the other relation is scanned to  
find matching tuples. This technique is preferred over nested-loop and sort-merge joins for several reasons:                                    

  1 Efficiency: Hash join can significantly reduce the amount of data that needs to be scanned, especially when the relations are large. By     
    partitioning the build relation into smaller pieces, the join can be performed more efficiently.

    the relations increases.
